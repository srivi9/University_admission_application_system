UB Rhinos Unveil New Kit by Elite Physiotherapy & Major Muscle and Fitness

University of Botswana (UB) Rhinos Rugby Club unveiled new kits for both its men's and women's teams at the UB Indoor Sports Arena on 19th April 2024. Elite Physiotherapy Clinic has sponsored the men's kit while Major Muscle and Fitness sponsored the women's team kit. Acting Director, Department of Culture, Sports and Recreation, Dr Comfort Mokgothu, expressed gratitude to the sponsors during a short ceremony held at the UB Campus Indoor Sports Centre.
